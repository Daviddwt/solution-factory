# Company Capability Taxonomy

This taxonomy is the first shared vocabulary for capability matching. It should be refined by reviewing whitelisted internal materials.

## Platform Layer

- 敢为云平台总览
- IOC 大屏与运营中心
- 业务中台
- 数据汇聚与分析
- 统一身份认证与安全管理

## Product And Engine Layer

- 敢为·瀚海低代码平台：表单、工作流、联动规则、移动端、快速业务搭建。
- 敢为·磐石物联网平台：多协议接入、设备感知、边缘协同、规则预警。
- 敢为·晨星可视化平台：GIS、BIM/3D、数字孪生、可视化大屏。
- 敢为·灵哲 AI 智能体：自然语言交互、多智能体协同、问数、知识库问答、业务代办。

## Scenario Layer

- 智慧园区
- 后勤综合管理
- 资产与资源管理
- 物业与服务工单
- 安全与应急管理
- 能源与设备管理
- 智慧社区
- 智慧工厂
- 智慧水务
- 智慧楼宇

## Fit Levels

- `existing`: Existing product or proven baseline capability.
- `configurable`: Can be configured by low-code, forms, workflows, rules, visualization, or dashboards.
- `integration`: Requires integration with customer systems, devices, or data platforms.
- `custom_dev`: Requires project-specific development.
- `unclear`: Needs more evidence or product/technical confirmation.
